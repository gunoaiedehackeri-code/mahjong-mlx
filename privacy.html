Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Mahjong MLX ("we", "our", "us") operates a browser-based game at mahjong-mlx.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Username
  • Email address (created automatically as username@mahjong-mlx.app)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Tangled.com username (optional, for payouts)

2.2 Game Data

  • MMLX token balance (virtual in-game currency)
  • Game level and progress
  • Completed levels count
  • Exchange requests (MMLX to MLX conversions)

2.3 Technical Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: Track progress, save game state, calculate rewards
  • Authentication: Verify identity and secure accounts
  • Exchange Processing: Process MMLX to MLX conversions
  • Email Notifications: Send exchange confirmations
  • Analytics: Improve game performance and user experience
  • Fraud Prevention: Detect multiple accounts and suspicious activity

4. Data Storage

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for data storage:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure account management
  • Cloud Firestore: Game data and exchange requests
  • EmailJS: Email notifications for exchanges

All data is stored on secure Google Cloud servers with industry-standard encryption.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Session management (keeping you logged in)
  • Analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising (Google AdSense)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect game functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We share data with:

  • Firebase/Google: Authentication and database hosting
  • EmailJS: Sending exchange confirmation emails
  • Google AdSense: Displaying advertisements
  • Tangled.com: Processing MLX payouts (username only)

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active accounts: Indefinitely while you use the service
  • Inactive accounts: 2 years of inactivity before deletion
  • Exchange records: 5 years for compliance

8. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Portability: Export your data in JSON format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails

Contact us at: privacy@mahjong-mlx.app (placeholder - update with real email)

9. Children's Privacy

This game is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this service.

10. Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted passwords (Firebase Authentication)
  • HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Firestore security rules
  • Email verification requirements
  • Rate limiting on exchanges

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
